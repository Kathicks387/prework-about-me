Everything Stokley

Stokley Williams, (born July 15, 1967), is known to his fans simply as Stokley! He is a master of several instruments including percussion, bass guitar, guitar, drums and keyboard. Stokely is also gifted in song writing, but nothing compares to his fabulous voice! His voice tones range from bass to mezzo-soprano with absolute perfection.

Stokley began his career in the 1980s as the lead vocalist and drummer of the Minneapolis band Mint Condition. The band was discovered by Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis in 1989 as they played at the prominent First Avenue nightclub in Minneapolis as they impressed them with a fusion of jazz, hip hop, funk and dance. Their debut album, "Meant to Be Mint", stayed on the charts for 34 weeks with the song, "Breaking My Heart (Pretty Brown Eyes)". Their second album, "From The Mint Factory (1993)" peaked at #2 on Billboard's Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart for four weeks straingt in the spring of 1994 with the hit single "U Send Me Swingin".

ln 2017 Stokley ventured out on his own with the debut of his solo cd "Introducing Stokley". He left his followers once again with his succulent, soultry blend of jazz, rhythm & blues, hip-hop and dance. Quavering with flawless and exquisite exellence songs as "Organic" and Level which were numbers 3 and five on billboard charts. Stokely gave his fans what they longed for; good and funky music. Stokely wasn't at all finished because in 2019 he released his single "She". Although the cd is not scheduled for release until the fall of 2020, She has lsnded #1 on the Billboard Charts for multiple weeks. I can't wait for the cd to be released!
